# Ortskasse — Investor Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title
**Ortskasse — Netflix für dein Dorf**
Subscription-based local commerce membership for DACH villages

## Slide 2: Problem
- Local businesses lose customers to online/chains
- Rural villages lose economic vitality
- Existing solutions (local currencies, loyalty programs) are complex and don't scale
- 72% of SME digitalization projects fail due to lack of adoption

## Slide 3: Solution
- Monthly subscription (9.90-24.90/mo) = instant 15-25% discount at ALL local businesses
- No vouchers, no points — scan card, save money
- Municipality gets real-time economic dashboard + Dorffonds
- Businesses pay nothing — trade margin for guaranteed foot traffic

## Slide 4: Market
- 11,000+ Gemeinden in DACH region
- 2,000+ villages with 500-5,000 residents (sweet spot)
- Hyperlocal services market: $2.9B → $3.3B (15.4% CAGR)
- TAM: 2,000 villages × €30k/year = €60M ARR potential in DACH

## Slide 5: Business Model
- 3 tiers: €9.90 / €14.90 / €24.90 per month
- Revenue split: 70% platform / 20% Gemeinde / 10% Dorffonds
- Break-even per village: ~45 subscribers
- Target per village: 300 subscribers = ~€30k/year

## Slide 7: Unit Economics
- CAC: ~€5-10 (word-of-mouth + Gemeinde newsletter)
- LTV: €150+ (avg 12+ months retention at €12.90/mo)
- LTV/CAC ratio: 15-30x
- Gross margin: ~85% (SaaS + payment processing fees only)

## Slide 8: Scaling
- Marginal cost per village drops from €5k (pilot) to €200 (village 50+)
- Template model: same code, new village = new tenant
- Distribution moat: Gemeinde partnership = trusted channel
- Network effects: more subscribers → more businesses → more subscribers

## Slide 9: Competitive Advantage
- Gemeinde co-ownership = distribution moat (no competitor gets Gemeinde newsletter)
- No payment license needed (identification-based, not payment-based)
- Cooperative DNA of DACH = cultural fit
- Data insights for municipalities = political buy-in
